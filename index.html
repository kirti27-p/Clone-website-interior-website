An Interior designer is the person who balances aesthetic considerations with utility with present structural planning which reflects the owners (Clients) life style. The designer has the clear vision for the designing space which is going to convert into office or residential purpose to its own beauty an Interior helps you to bring your vision of home or office or any other purpose space to lite or reality with consideration of space planning. designer trained eye and creative mind allow lot of solutions that clients can never imagined. designers vision plans and creates space in a way that makes the room both beautifull as well as functional too.

If you involve the designer in the early stage of house building it will surely save your money, which is also important factor for having an Interior designer.

An Interior designer also helps to cultivate relationship with vendors and other working persons who involves in executing the designs.

An Interior designer helps you to make your space into green space, which allows you to use of renewables on energy efficient things to make the space make the space or home or office more eco-friendly. an Interior designer helps you to make the space more workable create more space for work and creates the work environment too.

What is an interior

An Interior is the technique to convert the space into functional and aesthetically well manner. Interior designing means to plan the space and execute into reality. Interior designing means to have the knowledge of material, texture, colour, weight and much more other thing to be used.

Interior designing is generally splits into two types

  • Residential interior designing.
  • Commercial interior designing

In India Interior designing has been started before 17th century. The references of vishwakarma (one of the gods in Indian mythology) also shows such type of working in past, which till going carpenters & other artists worship them & treated as their god.

Interior designing means to improve the productivity too by making the work environment on the floor. It also helps to change the moods by creating lighting effects. Interior designing means to work with human pschycology.

An Interior designing helps you for creative design solutions which support for health, safety and to enhance the quality of life.

    An Interior designing helps you for creative design solutions which support for health, safety and to enhance the quality of life.

    • Material:
      Interior design materials and finishes are those things which make ups the finishes design. Finding the correct material or finish will make the difference in interior designers work and the ordinary one. In interior designing some basic materials are as given below which again diversify into a huge material or finish list. These are the basic things which are done while designing the space.
    • Flooring
    • Furniture
    • Ceiling
    • Lighting
    • Colour
    • Tapestry

    Flooring means to create a walking surface. Material classified as floor covering includes – ceramic tile, Natural stones, carpet, rugs, linoleum vinyl flooring, wood flooring and different seamless chemical floor coating.

    Furniture means to create space of utility and to decor the elements. This requires plywood, Natural wood, Laminates veneers, M. D. F. And various other types of boards, glass laminates and veneers and natural wood are finishing material of furniture which enhance the beauty of design.

    Ceiling means to create the imagining ceiling which makes more lighting effects and colour scheme tool. For ceiling work we can use gypsm sheet. POP (Plaster of paris) wood, wave boards, grill boards of MDF and many other material depends on design.

    Lighting is the major part of interior designing which creates moods & increases the work efficiency too. For lighting now a day LED are using throughout the fittings.

    Colours are major part to complete the designing work. Now a days water base paint are ottenly used to have ecofriendly environment. Main two parts of colours are interior paint & exterior paint. For interior paint – distemper, oil bond, oil paint, laster paints are generally use for exterior paint – cement paint, apex paint are used.

    • Tapestry:
      Tapestry involves fabrics. Natural fabrics are made of natural supplements which are expensive. Some of natural fabrics used to make fabric are linen, cotton, silk, jute, wool, cashmove, lorsehair. Artistitical fibers are generated from different engineering process. Acetate, rayon, triacetafe and modified rayou are some examples.
      Some of improved synthetic fibers are acrylic, nylon & polyester.
